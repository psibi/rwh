Hospitals in Chennai

Hospitals in Chennai provides great healthcare facilities to the people in the city as well as the citizens of India.

There are mainly two types of Hospitals:
  • General Hospital: This type of hospital deals with many kinds of injury and disease. A typical General Hospital has an Emergency Ward and has the capacity for emergency medical services. It has a number of beds for Intensive Care. It may also have specialized facilities for surgery and childbirth.

  • Specialized: This category includes trauma centers and children's hospitals. Specialized hospitals deal with specific special needs like psychiatric problems.
